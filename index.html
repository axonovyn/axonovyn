The Axonovyn platform

Axonovyn is not another general-purpose EMR. It is a focused layer for MS clinics and research teams to capture the data that matter most for progression, outcomes, and future AI models.

Data capture

Structured MS profiles

Capture core MS variables in a consistent format: diagnosis and MS type, DMT history, comorbidities, relapse history, and key imaging metadata (e.g., MRI/OCT dates and summaries).

Longitudinal tracking

Clinical, cognitive & PRO timelines

Track EDSS, walking and hand function measures, cognitive scores, and patient-reported outcomes such as fatigue, mood, and fear of relapse across visits.

AI-ready

Analysis-ready export

Generate de-identified, structured datasets suitable for statistical analysis and machine learning, including progression modeling and outcome prediction.

For MS clinics and centers

Built to support routine patient care while quietly organizing data in the background.

Clinical teams get:

  • A concise, longitudinal view of each patient’s journey.
  • Quick access to relapse history, disability, cognition, and treatment changes.
  • Consistent documentation to support quality improvement initiatives.
Designed to layer on top of existing workflows, not replace your EMR.

For researchers and AI teams

Axonovyn is designed with research questions in mind: progression, cognitive trajectories, and the impact of therapies over time.

Research teams gain:

  • Well-structured, de-identified datasets suitable for longitudinal analysis.
  • Variables aligned with modern MS research: disability, cognition, relapses, DMTs, PROs.
  • A foundation for integrating imaging and biomarker data in future phases.
We aim to support collaborations on AI models that are clinically grounded and deployment-ready.

About Axonovyn

Axonovyn emerged from academic work in MS, neuroimaging, and AI, with a simple observation: MS care generates rich data, but much of it is fragmented, unstructured, and under-used.

Our focus

We focus on building a lean, clinically meaningful data layer for MS care—one that supports everyday clinical decisions today and enables robust AI modeling tomorrow.

Our stage

Axonovyn is an early-stage, research-driven initiative. We are actively exploring pilot collaborations with MS clinics and academic centers interested in structured data capture and AI-ready infrastructure.
